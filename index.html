What's the story behind our NGO?

The founders understood that education was the key to unlocking opportunities and transforming lives.
The story of 13Foundation begins with a group of individuals who were deeply troubled by the pervasive inequality and lack of opportunities facing marginalized communities in their region. Many of them had personal experiences growing up in underserved areas or witnessing the struggles of friends and family members.

Driven by a shared desire to make a difference, they came together with a vision of creating an organization that would empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and build better futures for themselves. Drawing inspiration from their own journeys and the resilience of those they sought to help, they founded 13Foundation with a commitment to catalyzing positive change.
